# Sponge Boby
Interactive educational app for children aged 3-4 | NOIT 2019 National Finalist

## Available on Google Play
[Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=air.SpongeBoby)

## About
Educational app based on a kindergarten textbook, 
designed to introduce young children to the world around them.
All illustrations and audio were created by the development team.

##  Achievement
🥇 National Finalist — NOIT 2019 (National Olympiad in IT, Bulgaria)  
Score: Excellent

## Technologies
- Adobe Flash CS6
- ActionScript 3.0
- Adobe AIR (Android deployment)

## Team
Developed in collaboration with a partner.  
Responsibilities: programming, interaction design, audio production.
